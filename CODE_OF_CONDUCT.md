# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in qc2 to be welcoming, respectful, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while discussing ideas and code.
- Give actionable feedback and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus disagreements on the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing another person's personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sustained disruption, unwanted sexual attention, and publishing private information are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Scope

This code applies in the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and other spaces where someone represents the qc2 project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through a contact method listed on the repository owner's GitHub profile. For abuse that requires platform intervention, use GitHub's reporting and blocking tools.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Reports will be handled as privately and promptly as practical.
